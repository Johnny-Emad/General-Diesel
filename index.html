About General Diesel

General Diesel — Hany Soliman & Partners is an Egyptian company specializing in the import and distribution of genuine diesel injection pump spare parts. We supply workshops, fleet operators, and distributors across Egypt with original components from the world's most trusted manufacturers.

Our product range covers everything needed to repair and maintain diesel fuel injection systems — plungers, delivery valves, injector nozzles, rotor heads, and complete pump assemblies. Every part we carry is sourced directly from authorized international manufacturers, ensuring quality you can rely on with every order.

Hany Soliman

Founder & Managing Partner

With over 25 years of hands-on experience in the diesel fuel injection parts trade, Hany Soliman built this company on one principle: give every technician and workshop the right part, at the right time, from a name they can trust.
